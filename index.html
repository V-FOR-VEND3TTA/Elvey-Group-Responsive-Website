About Us

A Member of the Hudaco Group, Elvey is passionate about building strategic relationships with local and international manufacturers to provide products that meet the specific needs of local businesses and communities countrywide.

We achieve this through our wide range of solutions, technical support, supply chain expertise and expansive distribution network.

Our superior security solutions are reliable and easy to maintain We are with our customers every step of the way – offering consultations, training, technical support and on-site assistance.

Through our comprehensive training courses, each customer or installer gains the appropriate knowledge, skill and certification, to competently install and maintain security solutions.

Blue Label Accreditation

Each installer that commits to the courses on our selected brands on offer will get Blue Label Trained Installer accreditation, which will be listed on our lead distribution list. All trainees have the choice of becoming a DSC, Impro Access Control or a CCTV trained Blue Label Installer.

** Please note that in order to do the DSC, Optex or Networx training, it is mandatory to complete the Intruder Alarms foundation course first. **.
